Mission

Provide a free, engaging, and accessible resource that supports teachers, students, and parents in building safe and compliant classrooms.

Learning Objectives

  • Identify compliance rules
  • Apply classroom strategies
  • Demonstrate safe practices
  • Reflect on personal responsibility

Needs Analysis

Classrooms need clear expectations, student ownership, and family partnership. This site curates ready‑to‑use examples and tools.

Assessment

Short quizzes, role‑play evaluations, and reflection activities guide continuous improvement.

Training Session: Understanding Compliance in the Classroom

  1. Introduction (10): Welcome, objectives, quick poll.
  2. Why Compliance Matters (15): Definition, examples, demo video.
  3. Interactive (20): Role‑plays and guided reflection.
  4. Strategies (20): Teacher clarity, positive reinforcement, restorative moves; student norms; family partnership.
  5. Assessment & Feedback (15): Short quiz, “One strategy I will implement…”, group share.
  6. Closing (10): Recap and resources.

Safety Tips for Parents

  • Ask daily: “What does a safe classroom feel like?” Share one example.
  • Review device privacy settings together.
  • Practice a bullying response plan (who to tell, what to say).
  • Keep two emergency contact numbers in the backpack.
  • Use Creative Commons/open resources and model attribution.
